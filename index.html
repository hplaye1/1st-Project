Covid-19

We are open for orders only! We currently do not have items in our case and will we not be scheduling any walk-in events until further notice. If you would like to place an order, please give us a call at 225-448-5216 or email us at Tredicibakery@gmail.com to place your order and schedule your pick-up time. We are currently asking for one weeks notice for all orders. If you are ordering a custom cake, more time may be required to fulfill your order. We do not have any extra items for a quick pickup. All items are made to order only. If you need to cancel your order because of Covid, we will not give any refunds, however, we will give you credit for any future orders.

About

Tredici Bakery is a specialty bakery located in the heart of Baton Rouge's Mid City district. We specialize in airy French macarons, stunning wedding cakes, decorated sugar cookies, and seasonal pastries. All of our products are made in small batches by hand, using only the best ingredients.
